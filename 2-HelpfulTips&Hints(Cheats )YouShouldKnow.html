If you love cute life simulation games you’ll love playing Toca Life: World as it incorporates a wide variety of popular Toca Life titles in a single game. If you’re looking for helpful tips and hints to get the most out of playing Toca Life: World continue reading to discover a handy guide full of invaluable tips. Better yet you’ll also learn about a few cheats that will help you uncover different secrets in different sections of the game’s expansive map.

Play Toca Life: World at least once a week in order to receive a weekly gift.
Once a week you’ll be rewarded with a free gift. So it’s definitely worth ensuring that you check on your game at least once per week in order to ensure that you don’t miss out on incredible gifts. To claim your free gift head to the post office which is located in Bop City and click on the gift symbol.

Locate the hidden lair in the hospital.
Toca Life: World features countless secrets that are waiting to be discovered. As an example, you’ll be able to explore a mad scientist’s mysterious hidden lair which is located within the hospital grounds. Just be warned that even when you find the entrance to the mad scientists’ lair you’ll still figure out how to get inside which you may find challenging.

Place hats on your pets in order to stack them.
If you place hats on all of your pets you’ll be able to stack them on top of each other. You may want to see how many pets you can stack on top of each other.

Discover strange new animals in the park.
If you’d love to discover weird wonderful new animals head to the park and search for a tree branch that looks out of place. Each time that you click on it an egg containing a rare creature will fall to the ground. As there is a wide variety of animals which you can hatch it’s well worth clicking on the tree branch a couple of times to hatch a couple of different creatures.

Activate the secret dance floor in the park.
While you’re exploring the park you can also dance on a secret dance floor. To activate it simply click on the secret button which is located right underneath the radio. Instantly a disco ball will drop down and you’ll get to take part in a fun dance party.

Find the secret hideout which is located in the park.
The park is full of secrets and there is a small hill in the park which features a few bushes. Click on the bushes to see inside the hideout inside the hill. Inside the hideout, you’ll find a hiding spot for small objects and pets.

Encourage your dirty pets to take a dip in your paddling pool.
If you don’t have the time to sponge your dirty pets clean just encourage them to take a dip in your paddling pool instead of in order to save yourself time. Especially as no matter how many times you clean your pets, they’ll keep playing in the dirt and mud.

Experiment with creating new types of pet food.
If you visit the pet cafe you’ll be free to create new types of pet food. For example, you’ll be able to serve your pets a sandwich made out of dog food or a salad which is packed full of worms. Once you’ve served your pets your new creations watch their reactions to see if they enjoyed your food or not.

Head to the city district to hunt for hidden treasures.
When you’re exploring the city district ensure to hunt for hidden treasures. As you’ll be surprised at just how many treasures are hidden within the city. Some of which are more challenging to find than others. If you struggle to find treasures on your own simply read the Life Weekly in order to discover helpful clues to the locations of a few treasures.

So if you love spending countless hours playing cute open world life simulation games such as Toca Life: World ensure to test out some of the tips and tricks which are listed above. In order to get the most out of your game.
